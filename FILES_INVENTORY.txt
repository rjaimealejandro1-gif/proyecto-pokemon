4. damage-calculator.service.ts (53 lines) - src/app/core/engine/
   Purpose: Calculate damage between cards
   Methods:
   - calculateScaledHp() - HP * level
   - calculateScaledAttack() - ATK * rarity multiplier
   - calculateFinalDamage() - full formula with type advantage
   
   Formula:
   1. scaledAttack = baseAttack * rarityMultiplier
   2. defenseReduction = defense * 0.5
   3. baseDamage = max(10, scaledAttack - defenseReduction)
   4. finalDamage = baseDamage * typeMultiplier
   5. finalDamage = clamp(0, finalDamage, 1500)
   
   Rarity multipliers:
   - COMMON: 1.0
   - RARE: 1.2
   - EPIC: 1.5
   - LEGENDARY: 2.0
   
   Status: Working ✓
